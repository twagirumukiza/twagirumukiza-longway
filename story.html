Story

The narrative behind the work

A painting is never limited to what it shows. It also holds hesitation, impulse, tension, and silence. This page offers a slower, more reflective space for looking.

Each work carries a visual memory, a suspended emotion, and a vibration that remains beyond the first glance.

Through these series, twagirumukiza builds a visual language shaped by contrast, material depth, and shifts in perception.

Art becomes a way of inhabiting the world, revealing both its fractures and its discreet intensities.
